# Profile Picture

Add your profile picture here!

## File Name:
**profile.png** or **profile.jpg**

## Requirements:
- **Format**: PNG or JPG
- **Recommended size**: Square image (e.g., 500x500 pixels or 800x800 pixels)
- **Aspect ratio**: 1:1 (square)
- **File size**: Keep under 300KB for faster loading

## Tips for Best Results:
1. **Use a professional photo** with good lighting
2. **Center your face** in the frame
3. **Use a clean background** or blur it
4. **Smile!** ðŸ˜Š
5. The image will be displayed as a **circle**, so make sure your face is centered

## How to Prepare Your Photo:
1. Crop your photo to a square (1:1 ratio)
2. Ensure your face is in the center
3. Use good lighting and a clean background
4. Save as **profile.png** or **profile.jpg**
5. Place it in this folder

## Online Tools to Edit:
- **Remove background**: remove.bg
- **Crop to square**: photopea.com (free Photoshop alternative)
- **Compress image**: tinypng.com
